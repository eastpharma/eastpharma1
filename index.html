Product Lines

Components That

Our portfolio covers critical pharmaceutical packaging and preservation components. Each product undergoes rigorous quality control to ensure it meets GMP and international standards before reaching your production line.

Sealing Components
Flip-Off Caps

Aluminum and polypropylene flip-off seals engineered for injectable vials. Available in a full spectrum of colors with food-grade, non-toxic coating — ensuring a tamper-evident, sterile barrier for every application.

Available in 20mm size — standard pharmaceutical vial seal Full color palette with custom branding options Food-grade, non-toxic printing & varnishing certified Tested to ISO 8362-6/8872 force specifications Tamper-evident lacquered aluminum construction
Desiccant — Preservation
Silica Gel Capsules

Pharmaceutical-grade silica gel desiccant capsules for solid oral dosage containers. Each canister maintains moisture below critical thresholds to protect tablet and capsule integrity throughout shelf life.

Suitable for tablet & capsule bottles Controlled bead size and shape for optimal performance Tested for moisture adsorption capacity Pore structure verified via advanced analysis Marked "Desiccant — Do Not Eat" per safety standards
Desiccant — Moisture Control
Silica Gel Sachets

Flexible sachets containing pharmaceutical-grade silica gel beads, suitable for diverse packaging formats including blister packs, pouches, and secondary packaging. Engineered for consistent moisture absorption.

Multiple gram weights for varied humidity loads High-permeability packaging for fast adsorption Tested under controlled humidity conditions Verified across multiple drying & reuse cycles Suitable for blister, pouch and secondary packaging

01
Dust-Free Manufacturing Environment

All windows remain sealed, outside flooring is cement-paved to minimize contamination. All material stored on pallets. Units fitted with door closers, insect killers, and CCTV cameras.

02
Comprehensive SOPs at Every Stage

Standard Operating Procedures are documented and displayed for all production processes. Pest control performed bi-weekly with comprehensive treatments every five months.

03
Flip-Off Seal Quality Control

Automated image processing verifies crimping quality and print alignment on every batch. Force testing confirms removal force meets ISO 8362-6/8872 limits, with a minimum detachment prevention force of ≥6N to ensure security during transport.

04
Silica Gel Quality Verification

Raw silica sand tested for purity and uniformity before production. Temperature, pH, and drying conditions monitored in-process. Bead size, shape, and porosity verified via advanced analysis techniques.

05
Approved Vendor Database & Traceability

All incoming materials verified, inspected, and approved by QC with receiving inspection reports filed. Computerized approved vendor database updated continuously.
